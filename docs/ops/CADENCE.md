# Documentation and Planning Cadence

**Document type**: Operations policy  
**Doc ID**: OPS-CADENCE  
**Status**: Living v0.1  
**Source repo**: dcorps-docs-public (`docs/ops/CADENCE.md`)

> Scope: Update cadence for the roadmap, backlog, and decision tracking.

---

## 1. Roadmap cadence

- Review and refresh phases monthly.
- Update `docs/roadmap/INDEX.md` current status at least monthly or after major milestones.

---

## 2. Backlog cadence

- Update `docs/ops/TODO.md` weekly.
- Keep dependencies and owners current.
- Close or merge duplicate tasks within 7 days of discovery.

---

## 3. Decision log cadence

- Add entries to `docs/master/DECISION_LOG.md` at the time a decision is made.
- Include links to impacted documents or repos.

---

## 4. Changelog cadence

- Update `docs/master/CHANGELOG.md` for any versioned master changes.
- Release notes should be captured in the relevant repo where code lives.
