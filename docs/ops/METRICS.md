# Phase Success Metrics

**Document type**: Operations metrics  
**Doc ID**: OPS-METRICS  
**Status**: Living v0.1  
**Source repo**: dcorps-docs-public (`docs/ops/METRICS.md`)

> Scope: Measurable success metrics for each roadmap phase.

---

## Phase 0A - Public website finalization

- All public pages include publish/update dates.
- All public pages link to relevant docs where applicable.
- Roadmap page includes a high-level disclaimer.

---

## Phase 0 - Pre-mainnet readiness

- Public testnet uptime >= 95% over 30 days.
- At least one upgrade rehearsal on testnet.
- Two independent parties reproduce example entity reports from raw data.
- Bug bounty program active with published workflow.

---

## Phase 1 - Mainnet launch (Kernel v1)

- Mainnet produces stable blocks for 90 days with >= 99% uptime.
- First 10 entities register and publish reproducible reports.
- Reference explorer and indexer fully operational with public endpoints.

---

## Phase 2 - Operational completeness

- Conformance test suite published and used by at least one external team.
- API surfaces and schemas versioned and stable for 6 months.
- Monitoring and incident runbooks exercised in a controlled rehearsal.

---

## Phase 3 - Ecosystem bootstrapping

- At least three independent apps integrate with the Hub.
- IBC stablecoin flow achieves consistent daily settlement with no major outages over 60 days.
- Registry contains at least five listed modules or apps with clear risk labels.

---

## Phase 4 - Adapter layer

- At least one jurisdiction adapter module active with public documentation.
- At least one attestation module active with dispute/correction flows.
- Entities can attach adapters without altering kernel history.

---

## Phase 5 - Fully operational maturity

- Validator set >= 100 with no single operator > 5% voting power.
- Multiple independent indexers in production.
- Governance participation >= 25% voting power in regular proposals.
