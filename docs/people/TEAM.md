## Team coverage (hypothetical)

dCorps is currently founder-led. A complete execution team would typically include additional contributors across:

- Protocol engineering (Cosmos SDK, module design, upgrades).
- Cryptography and protocol security review (key safety, signing posture, formal/security review where applicable).
- Security engineering (threat modeling, audits, incident readiness).
- Data and indexing (indexers/explorers, reproducible views).
- Frontend and product (operator console, verification UX).
- Design (brand/graphic design, UI/UX, information design).
- Content and SEO (site information architecture, search visibility, technical writing).
- DevOps/SRE (environments, monitoring, releases).
- Governance, policy, and legal coordination (in collaboration with independent counsel and external partners).
- Ecosystem support (documentation, integrations, partnerships).
- Communications and public presence (social media, PR, events/speaking, community, go-to-market planning).
- Finance and operations (accounting, treasury operations, vendor management).
- Token and digital-asset operations (if applicable; illustrative: ICO/IDO): distribution planning, exchange/liquidity operations, compliance coordination, and investor relations—without implying any sale, listing, or return.
