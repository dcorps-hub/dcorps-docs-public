## Reader paths (choose your depth)

**If you are new to dCorps**

- Start with `docs/public/whitepaper/EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY.md`.
- Then read `docs/public/whitepaper/LITEPAPER.md`.

**If you run a nonprofit or support nonprofits**

- Start with `docs/public/whitepaper/NONPROFIT_NOTE.md`.
- Then read `docs/public/whitepaper/WHITEPAPER.md` sections on nonprofits, reporting, and disclosure modes.

**If you want the official public whitepaper**

- Read `docs/public/whitepaper/WHITEPAPER.md` (mid-length, designed for a full read).
- Optionally, use `docs/public/whitepaper/WHITEPAPER_LONG.md` for the long version.

**If you are a builder or integrator**

- Read `docs/public/technical/TECHNICAL_OVERVIEW.md`.
- Then use `docs/public/technical/INTEGRATION_GUIDE.md`.
- For normative behavior and schemas, use the specs under `docs/spec/`.

**If you are a validator**

- Read `docs/policy/POL-VALIDATORS.md`.
- Then skim `docs/security/SECURITY-POLICY.md` and `docs/security/INCIDENT-RESPONSE.md`.

**If you are a jurisdiction, institution, or legal professional**

- Read `docs/public/whitepaper/WHITEPAPER.md` sections on boundaries, governance, and legal position.
- Then read `docs/public/legal/RISK_DISCLOSURE.md` and `docs/public/legal/DISCLAIMERS.md`.

**If you are an investor or ecosystem partner**

- Read `docs/public/investor/INVESTOR_BRIEF.md` (informational, non-offer).
- Then read `docs/public/investor/TOKENOMICS_SUMMARY.md` and `docs/token/TOKEN-POLICY.md`.
