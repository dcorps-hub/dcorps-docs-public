# Legal Structure Path (BVI to Switzerland)

**Document type**: Legal summary  
**Doc ID**: LEGAL-STRUCTURE-PATH  
**Status**: Final v0.1  
**Source repo**: dcorps-docs-public (`docs/legal/STRUCTURE_PATH.md`)

> Scope: Summary of the intended legal structure path described in the Master Reference.

---

## 1. Current posture (early phase)

- Development corporation incorporated in BVI or similar jurisdiction.
- Focused on protocol engineering and integration services.
- No custody or financial intermediation role.

---

## 2. Transition posture (maturity phase)

- Establish a nonprofit foundation in Switzerland or a comparable jurisdiction.
- Transfer stewardship of shared standards and public goods to the foundation.
- Retain protocol neutrality and reduce single-vendor dependence.

---

## 3. Triggers and readiness signals

- Public testnet artifacts and reproducible tooling published.
- Governance processes operational with clear thresholds.
- Ecosystem contributors beyond the founding team are active.

---

## 4. Change control

Any material change to the legal structure path must be documented in governance proposals and recorded in internal decision tracking.
