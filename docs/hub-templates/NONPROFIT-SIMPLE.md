## Summary

- **Code**: NONPROFIT-SIMPLE
- **Template class**: Hub nonprofit
- **Complexity**: Low

---

## Intended for

- Smaller NGOs or single-program operators.
- Teams that need clear board governance and baseline transparency.

---

## Core structure

- **Governance**: Board governance with role wallets and clear authority mapping.
- **Wallets**: Canonical donation and program wallets.
- **Allocation categories**: Program vs overhead at minimum.
- **Events**: Tagged inflow/outflow events for reproducible allocation views.

---

## Reporting view

- Allocation view derived from tagged on-chain events over any selected timeframe.
- Minimum transparency floor across disclosure modes.

---

## Optional add-ons

- Document anchors for board decisions and material disbursements.
- Optional program detail if it improves clarity.

---

## Boundaries

- Templates define on-chain structure and evidence. Legal recognition remains off-chain or via optional modules.
