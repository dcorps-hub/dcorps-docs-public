## Summary

- **Code**: CORP-COMPLEX-PRIVATE
- **Template class**: Hub corporation
- **Complexity**: High

---

## Intended for

- Private corporations with multi-class units and committee governance.
- Multi-entity holdings or group structures that require stronger treasury policy.

---

## Core structure

- **Cap table**: Multi-class units with class-specific rights and voting rules.
- **Authority**: Committee approvals and protected matters for sensitive actions.
- **Treasury**: Advanced treasury policies and stricter approval routing.
- **Holdings**: Support for multi-entity or group structures where used.

---

## Reporting view

- Cash-based time-window view derived from tagged on-chain events.

---

## Optional add-ons

- Expanded base units for additional precision.
- Document anchors for material actions and supersession chains.

---

## Boundaries

- Templates define on-chain structure and evidence. Legal recognition remains off-chain or via optional modules.
