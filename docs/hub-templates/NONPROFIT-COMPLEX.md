## Summary

- **Code**: NONPROFIT-COMPLEX
- **Template class**: Hub nonprofit
- **Complexity**: Medium to high

---

## Intended for

- Umbrella NGOs or multi-entity sponsors.
- Organizations that require designated funds and more advanced allocation controls.

---

## Core structure

- **Governance**: Board and committee approvals with explicit authority mapping.
- **Funds**: Designated funds (restricted/unrestricted) with clear separation.
- **Programs**: Umbrella program structures with multi-program allocation rules.
- **Disclosure**: Selective disclosure patterns with anchors while preserving category-level transparency.

---

## Reporting view

- Allocation view derived from tagged on-chain events over any selected timeframe.
- Minimum transparency floor across disclosure modes.

---

## Optional add-ons

- Document anchors for fund policy changes and material allocations.
- Additional program detail when it improves clarity.

---

## Boundaries

- Templates define on-chain structure and evidence. Legal recognition remains off-chain or via optional modules.
