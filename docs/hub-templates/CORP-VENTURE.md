## Summary

---

## Intended for

- Venture-backed teams requiring board approvals and structured governance.
- Organizations using unit pools and vesting schedules.

---

## Core structure

- **Cap table**: Default 10,000 base units with optional precision expansion later.
- **Authority**: Board approvals and higher governance thresholds for protected actions.
- **Pools**: Unit pools with vesting schedules and grant tracking.
- **Transfers**: Stricter transfer rules and approval routing.

---

## Reporting view

- Cash-based time-window view derived from tagged on-chain events.

---

## Optional add-ons

- Document anchors for board resolutions, financing documents, and material actions.
- Optional precision patterns for finer-grained allocations.

---

## Boundaries

- Templates define on-chain structure and evidence. Legal recognition remains off-chain or via optional modules.
